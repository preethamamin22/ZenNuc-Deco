Advanced Dual-Reagent Surface Cleansing System

In high-stakes molecular biology laboratories, genomics core facilities, and clinical diagnostic environments, the presence of trace DNA/RNA fragments can jeopardize weeks of research and compromise test accuracy. Standard cleaning methods often fall short, leaving amplicons intact and capable of causing catastrophic false-positive results during amplification.

ZenNuc™ Deco offers an advanced, multi-pronged approach that completely overcomes the shortcomings of traditional single-bottle market standards. Engineered specifically for high-sensitivity workflows — including PCR, qPCR, RT-PCR, and NGS library preparation — ZenNuc™ Deco completely eradicates nucleic acid residues, shredding them into completely non-amplifiable fragments within minutes.

Advanced Mechanism of Action & Kinetics

A precisely sequenced biochemical cascade engineered to deliver absolute nucleic acid neutralisation.

1

Substrate Activation

Solution A (Deco Meta) delivers localised Cu²⁺ ions to the targeted biological grid, destabilising structural capsid envelopes and preparing exposed nucleic acids for catalytic degradation.

2

Catalytic Cleavage

Solution B (Deco Rox) initiates a rapid Fenton-like reaction cascade, yielding powerful hydroxyl radicals right at the phosphodiester bonds — systematically fragmenting DNA and RNA chains.

3

Complete Inertness

Nucleic acid structures are permanently divided into minor, non-replicating fragments, fully eliminating any false-positive PCR templates. Blue-green tint confirms completion.

Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) Workflow

For consistent validation within GLP-regulated environments, follow the certified sequential decontamination workflow outlined below.

1

Preparation

Clear target surfaces. Don standard PPE including nitrile gloves, lab coat, and safety eyewear before beginning.

2

Activation

Uniformly spray Solution A (Deco Meta), then follow immediately with Solution B (Deco Rox). Sequential order is critical.

3

Incubation

Allow contact time of 2–5 minutes. A faint blue-green tint indicates the chemical activation reaction is proceeding correctly.

4

Finalization

Wipe with a lint-free tissue. Rinse with DI water or 70% Ethanol, then air dry. Surface is now fully decontaminated.
