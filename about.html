Our Story

Densukia International OPC Pvt. Ltd. is a science-driven organisation founded on the conviction that laboratory contamination represents one of the most underestimated threats to research accuracy, diagnostic reliability, and scientific integrity.

Operating from Ratnagiri, Maharashtra, we design, manufacture, and distribute advanced biological and chemical solutions purpose-built for the most demanding laboratory environments — from molecular diagnostics and forensic science to next-generation sequencing and clinical research.

Our product range, including ZenNuc™ Deco, ZenNuc™ Grid, ZenNuc™ Aura, and ZenNuc™ Hypo, represents the culmination of this mission — setting new benchmarks in nucleic acid decontamination, industrial cleaning, hygiene, and disinfection science.

Our Mission

Delivering World-Class Solutions for Critical Environments
